Notes

- The sequence must use standard single-letter amino acid codes (20 common residues).

- Position is 1-based (the first residue is position 1).

- The wildtype amino acid must match the residue at the given position in the sequence, otherwise an error will be returned.

- Prediction may take 10-20 seconds depending on sequence length and server load.

- Positive ddG indicates destabilization; negative ddG indicates stabilization.
